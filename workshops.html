Lotus Blossom Ideation and Mindful Innovation,” helps participants learn visual thinking techniques and ideation using the Lotus Blossom technique with a mindful, playful twist.

Bubble Tea Workshop

I usually have a milk tea with jelly bubbles and no sprinkles. It works well for me, and I drink it often. But what if I add a sprinkle or change the bubbles to the red ones - what does that do? We’ll apply that thinking to workshop design and facilitation then throw in an interesting twist.
by Dave Bales & Terry Haayema

Rationalising the Conflict

Your group hits an immovable conflict, tensions rise, Progress stops. Our go to of Consensus or Consent leaves everyone a bit meh! Everyone's clarified, no one objects — yet nothing moves.

- What if we stopped trying to resolve every tension?
- What if we separated clarity from alignment and progress from resolution?
- What if we truly bring curiosity and difference forward whilst still navigating towards a common goal?

Some disagreements should not be resolved.
A method that moves groups forward when consent can't—without escalating tension --- Real shared ground, not the illusion of alignment ---Progress without pretending everyone agrees ---

Movement that honours legitimate differences
By Alidad Hamidi and David Witney

The AI-Enabled Facilitator

Turning Human Wisdom into a Working Chatbot,” will help you capture the wisdom of great human facilitators… and turn it into your very own personalised AI facilitation coach, right there in the room

You’ll experiment with prompts, design the “facilitator brain,” and shape an AI assistant that can help you:
✨ Design workshops
✨ Plan activities
✨ Resolve tricky group moments
✨ Improve facilitation flow and structure

by Sam Bowtell

Facilitation Masterclass

Ready to stop running meetings and start leading engaging, results-driven sessions?

This intensive 90-minute workshop is your fast-track to becoming a masterful facilitator. We'll unlock the essential foundations of professional facilitation and integrate powerful game dynamics to transform any session—from routine check-ins to complex strategy meetings—into a high-energy, participatory experience.

Learn to confidently guide any audience, manage challenging group dynamics, and ensure clear outcomes every time. Elevate your facilitation skills and your professional impact.
by Peter Lee & James Zhang

Agile is Dead

But is it really? Or are we just stuck in a stale framing of agile that no longer resonates?

Join us to explore the dramatic demise of agile (cue thunderclap), and then flip the script. Agile isn’t dead! It’s just waiting for a fresh coat of paint and a new perspective.

Collaborate with fellow agile rebels to reframe agile in ways that make sense for today’s teams, orgs, and chaos. Let’s resurrect agile, but make it weird, make it useful, and make it ours.
by Alex and Sally Sloley

Community Open Space


As a community, we use the Open Space Technology format to create our own agenda and record the resulting discussion.

Then, all the most important issues to those attending will be included in the agenda.

All the issues raised and worked on are addressed by the participants best capable of getting something done about them.

All the most important ideas, recommendations, discussions, and next steps are recorded and shared back.

It moves you forward and helps you see what you’re actually capable of.
